Hello, Ninja!

"Naruto" is a Japanese manga series written illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. The series has received the Quill Award in 2006. The franchise is the fourth best-selling manga series in history, selling 250 million copies worldwide in 46 countries.
Naruto first adapted into an anime television series produced by Pierrot and Aniplex, which has a total of 220 episodes and first broadcast in Japan from 2002 to 2007.
